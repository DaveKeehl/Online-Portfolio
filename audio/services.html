Services

I offer multiple services: recording, editing, mixing, mastering and graphic design. However my niche is mixing. Recording (especially drums recording) is available only for local bands. You can hire me for single services or for partial and complete production cycles.

RECORDING

25 CHF / hour

If you decide to hire me to record you or your band, I’ll do my best to make the most out of the instruments you already own. That’s where expenses can be cut dramatically. If some of your instruments are not in a proper recording conditions, we’ll find a way to make it work nonetheless. I don’t like the mentality “fix it in the mix”, so I always try to push the musicians in order to capture their skills at their best.

EDITING

25 CHF / hour

You can hire me to edit any kind of audio material. From music, to podcasts to audio post-production.
When I edit a music performance, my goal is to make everything sound super tight without losing the human factor. Good editing can have a big impact on the end product and it’s usually where most of the self-produced band fail at.
This service also includes audio restoration and file tagging if needed.

MIXING

100 CHF / song

Ah, my greatest passion. There’s nothing I like more than mixing. The feeling I get while seeing a song coming to life is for me something truly special. If you hire me, I’ll create a product catered to your tastes and able to compete with other songs of the same genre. If you hire me to mix an album, I’ll do my best to create a cohesive piece of art with flow.

MASTERING

50 CHF / song

Even if you mixed your songs by yourself, it’s always good to leave the mastering to another person with a set of fresh ears. My goal during mastering is not to push a mix to its limit, but rather to create a product that meets today’s standards and that translates well across a wide variety of sound devices.
If needed, this service also includes DDP creation.

GRAPHICS

25 CHF / hour

I advertise myself as a sound engineer, but I’m actually also a graduated visual designer. I know what looks good (and what doesn't) and how to make something as such. If your hire me to create graphics for you, I’m able to offer you the following services:

  • CD and DVD artwork
  • CD and DVD case design
  • Streaming videos
  • Personal website
  • Business cards
  • Banners for social networks
  • Flyers
