Hey there!

Making music has never been easier. Even on a tight budget you can still buy incredible gear that even a few years ago was just fantasy. And this is great! Because music making shouldn’t be a game for rich people, but rather a universal language of self-expression.

However, the gap between amateur and pro audio is still there and often gets in the way, stopping you from achieving the results you have in mind. How many times have you wanted to go in a nice studio to record your songs only to discover that the rates were too high?

That’s exactly who I am. My name is Davide and I’m both a musician and a sound engineer. I have two goals. The first one is to be able to offer the same services to artists that don’t have access to high-end studios, while the second one is to prove wrong anyone who tells me I can’t do it.

If I have sparked your interest I hope you’ll take my work into consideration.
